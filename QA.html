The Story of Ayodhya King

The story of Lord Rama is told in the epic Ramayana, which is written by the sage Valmiki and is estimated to date back to around 200 B.C.E. The story is about the adventures of Rama, the god-king, and includes the following events: Rama's birth Rama was born in the city of Ayodhya to King Dasharatha and Queen Kaushalya. The gods intended for Rama to be born to defeat the demon king Ravana. Rama's marriage Rama won the hand of Sita, the daughter of a king, in an archery contest.
